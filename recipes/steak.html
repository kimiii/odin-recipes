Steak

Description

This is one of the best recipes for steak. Everyone loves it, everyone can cook, but this recipe makes the most common dish among mankind to a WOW dinner.

Ingrediants

  • A good piece of meat
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil on your fan
  2. Put the meat on it
  3. Melt the butter and grind the black pepper on it
  4. Tada
